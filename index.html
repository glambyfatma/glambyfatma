About Glam by Fatma

At Glam by Fatma, we believe beauty is an experience—not just a look. Based in Kitui, we specialize in makeup artistry, custom hairstyling, and elegant henna designs for brides, photoshoots, and special occasions across Kenya.

Whether you're preparing for your big day or simply want to shine in a moment that matters, our mobile and in-studio services are personalized to match your skin tone, style, and story. Every detail is thoughtfully crafted to ensure you feel confident, radiant, and unforgettable.
